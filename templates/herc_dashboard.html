What is the Kaiser Permanente HVA Tool?

The Kaiser Permanente Hazard Vulnerability Analysis (HVA) tool is a standardized approach for healthcare facilities to identify and assess potential hazards, threats, and events that could negatively affect operations. This tool is widely used in the Hospital Preparedness Program.
