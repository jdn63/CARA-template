About CARA

Comprehensive Automated Risk Assessment

What is CARA?

CARA is an open-source, geospatial public health and emergency preparedness risk assessment platform. It integrates authoritative data sources to produce multi-domain risk scores for jurisdictions at the county, district, province, or national level.

Risk scores are computed using the PHRAT (Public Health Risk Assessment Tool) quadratic mean formula, which weights each domain by policy relevance and produces a final composite score on a 0-1 scale. Scores drive prioritized action plans aligned with the {{ framework_name | default('applicable assessment framework') }}.

CARA is designed to support both routine strategic planning and operational emergency response. All data is pre-fetched and cached from authoritative sources on a scheduled basis; no external API calls occur during a user session.
